MARVEL

Iron Man

"Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional superhero appearing
in Marvel Comics. He's a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor
who designs and builds a high-tech suit of armor to escape captivity
and fight evil. The suit grants him enhanced strength, flight,
and various weapons, making him a powerful force for good. "

Captain America

"Captain America, also known as Steve Rogers, is a prominent Marvel Comics
superhero introduced in Captain America Comics #1 in 1941.
Originally a frail man, he was enhanced to the peak of human physical perfection
by an experimental "Super-Soldier Serum" during World War II. This gave him superhuman abilities like
strength, speed, durability, and a mastery of martial arts and tactical strategy. He wields a
virtually indestructible vibranium shield and wears an American flag-inspired uniform."

Hulk

"The Hulk, also known as the Incredible Hulk,
is a Marvel Comics superhero, specifically Bruce Banner,
who gains immense strength and green skin when angered or experiencing high
heart rate. He is a brilliant scientist who, after being exposed to gamma radiation,
transforms into the powerful, rage-fueled Hulk."

"Thor Odinson, the God of Thunder, is a powerful Asgardian warrior and a founding member
of the Avengers. He wields the mighty hammer Mjolnir and possesses incredible strength,
durability, and control over lightning and weather. Thor is known for his royal
lineage, initially as the prince of Asgard and later as its king. "

Hawkeye

"Hawkeye, whose real name is Clint Barton, is a master archer and marksman
in the Marvel universe. He is a skilled agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. and a founding
member of the Avengers. While not possessing superhuman abilities,
his exceptional accuracy and combat skills, combined with his use of advanced
weaponry, make him a formidable hero. "

Black Widow

"Black Widow, also known as Natasha Romanoff, is a highly skilled spy and assassin
who becomes a key member of the Avengers in the Marvel universe.
Born in Russia, she was trained from a young age in the Red
Room, a secret Soviet program that turned young girls into lethal
operatives. She is renowned for her expertise in espionage,
martial arts, and weaponry, particularly her Widow's Bite bracelets. "
